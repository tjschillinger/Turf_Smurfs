About Turf Smurfs

We believe that a good business is the sum total of the individal virtues practiced by the people who work within the business. To provide the best service, we hire only the best individals. Everything we do is based on these 4 core values:

  • Act with Integrity

    We do what we say, and say what we mean. Aiming to always act with transparency and honesty is what we believe makes us a great service provider and employer.

  • Commitment

    We always have the long-term in mind. Businesses have ups and downs, and we ignore the downs, knowing that our grit will have the final word.

  • Treat People like People

    We delivery good lawncare at a reasonable profit and treat our customers like human beings. That's why they will always come back for more.

  • Passion

    Passion is at the heart of our company. We are continuously moving forward, innovating, and improving.
